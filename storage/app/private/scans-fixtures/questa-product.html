Overview

Welcome to the era of the Gold Rush, where the thirst for wealth clouds the mind, and gold becomes the cause of the most dangerous adventures! You are a team of treasure hunters tasked with recovering stolen golden nuggets taken from the mine of a small town. The robbers have fled with the loot on a speeding train, and only you can stop them! Your mission is to sneak onto the train, overcome cunning traps, and solve the mysteries left behind by the criminals. Will you manage to reclaim the gold and become heroes, or will the train carry the treasure away forever?
